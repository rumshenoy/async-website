2015 International Show n' Tell on Lanyrd

Sponsored by Brandwatch.

Thursday, 5th November 2015 7:15pm @ 68 Middle St Brighton, BN1

Roll up folks, time for the annual International Async Show'n'tell! It's ffconf (formerly known as Full Frontal conference) on 6th November, and as usual we'll be hosting a pre-conference special Async for Brightonians and pilgrims alike to show us what's getting their juices flowing right now.

If you'd like to attend, just sign up on the Lanyrd event page.

And, if you’d like to grab a speaking slot, you’ll have five minutes on the night to tell us about something… a project, a library, a technique, a story, or anything else about JavaScript, related tech (HTML, CSS, SVG, etc), or the wider world of web. We hope to bring together a blend of local projects with those from travellers to England for the conference.

Speakers, please add a comment to the Speakers' Gist.

For a spot of inspiration, take a look at our International Show n' Tells in 2014 2013, 2012, 2011 and 2010. We've also hosted local show n' tells: 1 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7.

Update

This event will be sponsored by Brandwatch, so free beers & pizzas will be supplied! Please contact hello AT asyncjs DOT com if you have specific dietary requirements.

About:

Async meets monthly in Brighton & Hove, UK, to learn, discuss and play with JavaScript and related technologies.

We host talks, hack nights, show n' tells and community events, all of which are free.

Doors open 7.15pm, main session begins 7.45pm, we end around 9.30pm and continue nearby for an evening social.

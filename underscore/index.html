Using Underscore.js with jQuery on Lanyrd

Thursday, 24th November 2011 7:15pm @ The Skiff 6 Gloucester Street, Brighton, BN1 4EW

For this Async session, we are glad to present a talk from Dan Neame, a JavaScript engineer at Brighton-based startup Brandwatch, on working with Underscore.js and jQuery.

Underscore.js is a 'utility-belt library' and it makes it a breeze to solve many of the common problems you'll see when working with jQuery.

Dan will have some interactive examples to show how jQuery and Underscore can work together to save time and headaches. Wading through an AJAX response? There's a function for that... Laggy scroll listeners? There's a function for that too!

About:

Async meets monthly in Brighton & Hove, UK, to learn, discuss and play with JavaScript and related technologies.

We host talks, hack nights, show n' tells and community events, all of which are free.

Doors open 7.15pm, main session begins 7.45pm, we end around 9.30pm and continue nearby for an evening social.

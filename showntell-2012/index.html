2012 International Show n' Tell on Lanyrd

Thursday, 8th November 2012 7:15pm @ The Skiff 6 Gloucester Street, Brighton, BN1 4EW

It's that time of year again in Brighton, when the heady seaside summer makes way for the dark introspection of winter, alack.

But cheer up! We have the warm solace of the Full Frontal conference in our midst and, as ever, Async is here to get the ball rolling and the blood flowing. With arms wide open, we welcome our long-lost cousins from the exotic east, the nascent north, the way-out west, and the soulful south. This is the 2012 International Show n' Tell.

If you'd like to attend, just sign up on the Lanyrd event page.

About:

Async meets monthly in Brighton & Hove, UK, to learn, discuss and play with JavaScript and related technologies.

We host talks, hack nights, show n' tells and community events, all of which are free.

Doors open 7.15pm, main session begins 7.45pm, we end around 9.30pm and continue nearby for an evening social.

4th Birthday Show n' Tell on Lanyrd

Thursday, 19th June 2014 7:15pm @ 68 Middle St Brighton, BN1

Async is four years old! To celebrate, we are holding a (slightly belated) evening of 5-minute quickfire talks. Tell us about a project, a library, a technique, a story, or anything else to do with JavaScript and its extended family (HTML, CSS, SVG, animated GIFs) or the wider world of web.

If you're up for speaking, then please add a comment to the Speakers' Gist.

About:

Async meets monthly in Brighton & Hove, UK, to learn, discuss and play with JavaScript and related technologies.

We host talks, hack nights, show n' tells and community events, all of which are free.

Doors open 7.15pm, main session begins 7.45pm, we end around 9.30pm and continue nearby for an evening social.
